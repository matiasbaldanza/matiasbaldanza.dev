# Brief — matiasbaldanza.dev

## What

Personal homepage for Matías Baldanza plus an unlisted `/dotcms` mini-site: a
personal pitch for a Developer Relations role at dotCMS, modeled on Jason
Zimdars' 37signals application (jasonzimdars.com/svn/).

## Why

The CV sent to dotCMS prints `matiasbaldanza.dev/dotcms` as the application
URL. The site itself is part of the pitch: a DevRel candidate showing, not
telling — clean writing, clean code, public repo, honest commit history.

## Audience

- `/` — anyone: developers, recruiters, readers of future articles.
- `/dotcms` — one hiring manager and their team. Unlisted (noindex, not linked
  from the homepage); only people with the URL find it.

## Pages (v1)

1. `/` — photo, name, one-line positioning, social links, email. Structured to
   grow: article/content cards will be added over time.
2. `/dotcms` — the pitch: about me → about dotCMS → why I'm the right fit.
3. `/dotcms/resume` — HTML resume + download link to the exact PDF sent to
   recruiters.

A case study page (Zimdars had one for Backpack) is deliberately deferred past v1.

## Constraints

- Live on the real domain the same day this repo was created (~3h budget).
- Static output, zero client-side JS in v1.
- Design: typography-first, whitespace-heavy, one accent color.
- Wording on the resume page stays identical to the PDF.
